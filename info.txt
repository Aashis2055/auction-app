Auction app

Mobile app for auctioning (Buying and selling ) bike and vehicles 
Uses nodejs for backend, flutter for client app, react for admin panel, and mongodb for database

The app allows the sellers auction vehicles such as bike, car and buyers to bid on them
provides real time notification and price change
uses paypal for payment 

the app provides the prices range of bike, car by analyzing the factors such as (current model price, no of years used, km driven, no of people interested etc)

provides chat platform for buyers and seller to talk

users can report on a post and admins can ban or remove the post

social platform support (gmail login, sharing on facebook)
